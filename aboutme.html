About Me

Hi, I'm Daniel. I am a Korean-American from Massachusetts, attending the University of Maryland. I am a Computer Science and Mathematics double major, entering my third undergraduate year.

I have been coding for a long time, starting with basic block coding on Scratch with my best friend in 5th grade. I took all of the computer science courses offered at my high school, learning foundational skills such as HTML, Python, Java, OOP design, and more. I even earned a 5 on my AP Computer Science A exam!

I decided to continue my pursuit of computer science at UMD with a focus on cybersecurity.

At UMD, I am part of the Sigma Phi Delta Engineering Fraternity, where I serve as the treasurer of an executive board. Being treasurer has taught me many invaluable skills such as teamwork, communication, and leadership.

I even developed a Python auto-messenger to automate the process of communicating financial information, as I got quite sick of copy and pasting messages to 70+ members!

The University of Maryland and SigPhi are my happy places, and I love all of my friends and acquaintances who make every day worth it :D
